The enterprise problem

Enterprise travel decisions are fragmented across booking tools, pricing engines, policies, and human judgment. This fragmentation creates inefficiency at scale.

  • Inefficient routing
    Multi-city and complex itineraries degrade quickly without optimization.
  • Uncontrolled spend
    Policy, timing, and preference variables are rarely optimized together.
  • Decision fatigue
    Teams burn time comparing options instead of acting on outcomes.
  • Low consistency
    Distributed teams make inconsistent decisions across regions and roles.

The solution

TravelAir is being developed as an AI-native travel intelligence layer that sits above enterprise travel infrastructure.

Designed to:

  • Synthesize inputs
    Pricing, routing, policy, and preference signals.
  • Generate outcomes
    Optimized, decision-ready itineraries and routing logic.
  • Reduce overhead
    Lower planning time and cognitive load across teams.
  • Enable scale
    Consistency and optimization across distributed organizations.
