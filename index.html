FAQ

Common questions serious buyers ask when evaluating a premium domain / brand asset.

Is this a live product?

This is currently positioned as a premium brand asset (domain + narrative + acquisition-ready landing). A buyer can build a platform, use it for licensing, or integrate it into an existing product line.

Why does the name matter this much?

In travel, trust and memorability are everything. A short brand name improves click-through, recall, referrals, and long-term expansion. This is the same reason top travel brands are short and global.

What’s the clean transfer process?

Once terms are agreed, the domain is transferred to the buyer’s registrar account and the repository/assets are handed off. This landing can remain active during transition or be replaced by the buyer.

Can this be licensed instead of sold?

Yes — licensing can be structured for a buyer who wants to operate under the brand without a full acquisition upfront.
